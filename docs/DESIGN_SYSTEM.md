# Design System: Trillion-Dollar Law Firm Standard

## Visual posture

The interface should feel like a digital chamber, not a startup app. The visual tone is:

- institutional;
- restrained;
- sober;
- confident;
- premium;
- lawyerly;
- private;
- procedural.

## Palette

- Deep navy: authority and confidentiality
- Charcoal ink: legal seriousness
- Warm off-white/vellum: document atmosphere
- Muted brass/gold: institutional accent
- Thin borders: professional restraint

## UI principle

The system should never say, “Click here and get legal advice instantly.”

It should say, visually and verbally:

> “You are entering a controlled legal process.”

## Interaction principle

Every important step must clarify status:

- preliminary review;
- conflict check;
- consultation;
- engagement;
- filing instruction;
- matter room;
- closure.

## Anti-patterns to avoid

- playful illustrations;
- loud gradients;
- exaggerated claims;
- fake automation promises;
- casual “submit your case and we will file” language;
- payment-first language for filing fees;
- representing AI as the lawyer.
